About the Villa

Where glass meets
tropical living

Built in 2023 in the serene pocket of Kerobokan, Villa Ocearn is a fully glazed contemporary villa where architecture dissolves the boundary between inside and out. Polished cement floors, double-height ceilings, and floor-to-ceiling glass panels frame the tropical landscape in every direction.

Two levels. Five private suites. A chef's kitchen, entertainment room with pool table and bar, expansive pools, and private parking — all within walking distance of Bali's finest cafés and coastline.
